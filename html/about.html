About Us

Smart Telecom established in 2009 as a VoIP service provider and since then has been prominent and over the edge in the business. An idea was put forth in 2012; that we should be the one stop shop for internet and home phone and embarked on the DSL services in provinces across Ontario In 2012, with the introduction of the FIBER TO THE NODE (FTTN), we joined the community of Fiber Internet Providers to satisfy explicit needs of our customers. We also extend our services in the platter with Business Phone Service and Long Distance Calling. Smart Telecom we want to deliver smarter service and superior experience to our valued customers with our state-of-the-art Data Center located at one of the highly secure core datacenters available in North America with the latest and the greatest technology of the internet service sector. Our success is based on cost-effective and privileged services tailored for our customer’s specific needs thereby building long term partnership with our customers.

Handling more than 45 million world-wide minutes and business partnerships with industry leading carriers.
Ensuring excellent voice quality to power tune your communication structure with state-of-the-art servers and equipment’s at our data center.
Excellent and knowledgeable support staff that are trained and committed to fulfill the ever changing communication requirements of our existing and potential customers.

Unlike other internet service providers, we want to offer a complete innovative solution for even the silver surfer to experience a service that is as easy as a pie. Therefore, Smart Telecom in 2012 started offering Cable Internet in most part of Ontario and will soon be widening our cable internet services in rest of Ontario. Get connected with Smart Telecom and enjoy the benefits of a reliable and secure service at your home or business.
